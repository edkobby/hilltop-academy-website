Admission Process

At Hill-Top Academy International, we welcome students from diverse backgrounds who are eager to learn and grow. Our admission process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our nurturing and challenging academic environment.

Step 1: Application

Complete our online application form with all required documents including academic transcripts, recommendation letters, and personal statement.

1

Step 2: Assessment

Participate in our age-appropriate assessment designed to understand your child's academic level and learning style.

2

Step 3: Interview

Meet with our admissions team and teachers to discuss your child's interests, goals, and how Hill-Top Academy can support their growth.

3

Step 4: Decision

Receive your admission decision within 2 weeks. Successful applicants will receive enrollment information and next steps.

4

Age Groups & Requirements

Early Years (Ages 3-5)

  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Previous school report (if applicable)
  • Parent questionnaire

Entry Points: September, January

Primary School (Ages 6-11)

  • Academic transcripts from previous school
  • Teacher recommendation letter
  • English and Mathematics assessment
  • Student interview

Entry Points: September, January

Junior High (Ages 12-15)

  • Complete academic records
  • Two teacher recommendations
  • Comprehensive academic assessment
  • Student and parent interview

Entry Points: September only
