Who We Are

A Purposeful School Community in Accra

Hill-Top Academy International is a values-driven learning community based in Accra, Ghana. Since 2008, we have served families with an intentional approach to teaching that balances academic challenge with emotional and social growth.

We support learners from Creche through Kindergarten, Primary, and Junior High School (JHS 3). Our classrooms are structured, warm, and student-centered, with experienced educators who help each child build confidence and discipline.

Our focus is simple: prepare students for life beyond exams. We build foundational literacy and numeracy, critical thinking, leadership habits, and the character needed to thrive in a changing world.

Programs Overview

Learning Pathways at Hill-Top

Creche Ages 1-3

A warm early-years setting focused on safety, routines, social development, and joyful discovery through guided play.

Kindergarten Ages 4-5

Foundational literacy and numeracy, language growth, and confidence-building through interactive and structured learning.

Primary Basic 1-6

Strong core academics across English, Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies, with emphasis on creativity and critical thinking.

Junior High School JHS 1-3

Exam-ready preparation, leadership development, and life skills to help learners transition confidently to Senior High School.
